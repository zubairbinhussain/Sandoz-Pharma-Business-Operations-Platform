# Sandoz — Pharma Business Operations Platform

> Analytics & BI platform consolidating fragmented departmental data into one centralized intelligence system.

🔗 [sandoz.com](https://sandoz.com)

## Overview

Sandoz is a global leader in generic and biosimilar medicines dedicated to expanding access to affordable, high-quality healthcare worldwide. Led the complete development of Sandoz South Africa's business operations platform, engineering the full solution from data architecture through to frontend delivery. The platform consolidated fragmented data sources across departments into a single centralized system delivering real-time insights, interactive visualizations, and streamlined reporting.

## Problem

Sandoz South Africa's business operations were running on fragmented data systems spread across multiple departments with no centralized visibility. Decision-makers lacked access to real-time insights, relying instead on manual reporting processes that were slow, inconsistent, and difficult to act on. Marketing analytics, operational data, and business intelligence were siloed, creating blind spots that slowed strategic decision-making and limited the organization's ability to respond quickly to market changes.

## Solution

Designed and built a centralized business operations platform that consolidated all departmental data sources into a single, unified intelligence system. Integrated Datorama as the core real-time marketing analytics engine, enabling live data ingestion, processing, and visualization across business units. Used Moqups for UX prototyping and interface design to ensure the platform was intuitive for non-technical stakeholders. Delivered interactive dashboards, automated reporting, and real-time data visualizations that gave leadership and operations teams instant access to accurate, actionable business intelligence.
